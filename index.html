Welcome

Ogo Kerubu-Serafu Project is generally conceptualised for the major purpose of projecting, promoting and rebranding the peculiarities of the C & S Church, some of which are misunderstood by other comity of Churches and the general public, whereas those peculiarities are actually the basis of the Church and the manifestation of such is our Glory. Hence, the ‘OGO KERUBU-SERAFU’ PROJECT.

As members of Cherubim and Seraphim Church on earth, we have the mandate to fulfil on earth the mandate of the heavenly Cherubs and Seraphs. The earthly Cherubim and Seraphim are duty bound to exhibit in different manifestations the peculiar natures and assignment of the heavenly Cherubim and Seraphim as their counterparts on earth.

By this implication, C & S members are the glory of God on earth in the order of the heavenly Cherubs and Seraphs, and are expected to do all in capacities to exhibit and radiate this glory in all manners, and through diverse mediums and platforms. Hence, the conceptualization of ‘Ogo Kerubu-Serafu’ Project to contribute to the efforts to fulfil and replicate on earth the mandate of the heavenly Cherubim and Seraphim through continuous and series of activities expressed through diverse mediums and platforms such as TV/Radio shows, strategic campaigns, documentaries, revivals, philanthropic projects, Corporate Social Responsibility projects and especially, special events and programs.

About the Project

Our project serves as a strong universal platform for the renewal, projection, promotion and globalization of the original and Divine Spiritual Legacies of the C & S Church, as exemplified and symbolized by the Prophetic and Apostolic Ministry of Saint Moses Orimolade.

Vision Statement

To serve as a Strong Universal Platform for the Renewal, Projection, Promotion and Globalization of the original and Divine Spiritual Legacies of the C & S Church as exemplified and symbolized by the Prophetic and Apostolic Ministry of Saint Moses Orimolade so that the Church can take its position in the comity of Churches and fulfil its divine mandate at this end time period.

Mission Statement

To serve as a global medium for the rebranding, repackaging and documentation of the original doctrinal activities that are geared towards showing forth the Glory of God as the earthly Cherubs and Seraphs; achieving better corporate and spiritual image of C & S Church Worldwide.

Objectives

  • To serve as a medium to exhibit the peculiar nature and assignment of the heavenly Cherubic and Seraphic angels as their counterparts on earth.
  • To celebrate, magnify and glorify the God of C & S in our peculiar ways.
  • To further show forth the Glory of God as a Church, the Cherubim and Seraphim on earth.
  • To amplify, further project and promote the original and divine messages of Cherubim and Seraphim Church’s faith peculiarities, doctrines and doctrinal practices.
  • To serve as a medium of rebranding the Cherubim and Seraphim Church for better corporate and spiritual image as well as identity.
  • To create doctrinal legacies that transcend time.
  • To trailblaze the renewal of the prayer and Pentecostal Revival with other Spiritual values that C & S Church is known for.
  • To serve as a medium for a beacon of hope, inspiration and restoration for the Church.
  • To further foster stronger connections with other Churches and other parts of the body of Christ.
  • To serve as a watchdog that will curb all unbiblical practices that have given the Church a negative image.
  • To regenerate, revitalize, preserve, project and promote the legacies of Saint Moses Orimolade Tunolase, the Founder of C & S Church and the heritage of the faith dynasty he, and other of our fathers of faith, has left behind for us.
